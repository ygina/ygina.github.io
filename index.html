Gina Yuan

Keybase | Email | GitHub | CV

I'm a 5th year Computer Science PhD candidate at Stanford. I previously studied Computer Science at MIT (B.S.'19, M.Eng.'19) with a concentration in systems.

I am interested in building systems and designing algorithms for networks. My current research involves designing Internet middleboxes that can provide performance enhancements even when protocols (such as QUIC) are fully encrypted, as well as algorithms for path-aware congestion control or fast hash table lookups in the dataplane.
