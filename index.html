Gina Yuan

Email | GitHub | CV

I'm a final-year Computer Science PhD candidate at Stanford. My research is in computer networks, including transport protocols and the application of set reconciliation techniques to in-network proxies. I previously studied Computer Science at MIT (B.S.'19, M.Eng.'19).
